About H&K Getaways

Hi! We're Kenneth and Haley, the team behind H&K Getaways — and proud parents to our daughter who inspires our love for family travel.

As a family ourselves, we understand how important vacations are. Some of the best memories in life are made while traveling together, whether it's experiencing the magic of Disney for the first time or relaxing on a beautiful cruise ship while exploring new destinations.

We created H&K Getaways because we love helping people plan those unforgettable moments. From choosing the perfect Disney resort to finding the ideal cruise itinerary, we take care of the details so you can focus on enjoying the experience with the people you love.

Our goal is simple: make travel planning easy, stress-free, and exciting so your family can focus on what really matters — making memories that last a lifetime.
